Introduction

One of my favorite hobbies is playing golf. I enjoy it because it challenges both my mind and my body. Every game feels different. depending on the weather, the course, and my focus that day. Golf has taught me patience, discipline, and the importance of staying calm even when things don’t go as planned. I first started playing a few years ago and was drawn in by how relaxing and of a sport it can be. In most other sports where you have to hit a ball; the ball is flying quite fast at you, and you have such a small amount of time to react and hit the ball. In golf, the ball is still, and you have as much time as you would like to hit the ball. Yet Somehow, I would argue it is one of the hardest sports. This deception makes the sport super interesting to me, and I love it. It takes years of hard work. just to be average at the sport, and even then you have the most frustrating days where you feel like you have gotten so much worse. I think golf is the only sport where you can feel like you've improved drastically one day, And the next day you are back to being terrible.
